The overreaching objective is to make as many matches, and develop as high a score as possible.

Matches must be made by swapping a jewel with a neighbor in any of the vertical or horizontal cardinal directions. Matches may not be made diagonally.

A match is any continuous line of the same jewel consisting of a minimum of three jewels. You will be rewarded with points depending on how large your match is.

Matches may intersect horizontally and vertically.

The game is over when no more matches can be made.
